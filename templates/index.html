Frequently Asked Questions

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value calculated from your weight and height. It's a reliable indicator of body fatness for most people and is used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

Men and women have different body compositions and distributions of muscle and fat. Using gender-specific BMI calculators provides more accurate assessments and tailored health recommendations.

It's recommended to calculate your BMI every 3-6 months, or whenever there's a significant change in your weight or lifestyle. Regular monitoring helps track your health progress effectively.
