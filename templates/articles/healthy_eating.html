Healthy Eating Guidelines

Building a Balanced Diet

A healthy eating pattern is fundamental to maintaining a healthy weight and preventing various health conditions. Here are essential guidelines to help you make better food choices.

Key Components of a Healthy Diet

  • Fruits and Vegetables

    Aim for 5-9 servings daily, varying colors to get different nutrients

  • Whole Grains

    Choose brown rice, whole wheat bread, and oats over refined grains

  • Lean Proteins

    Include fish, poultry, legumes, and plant-based proteins

  • Healthy Fats

    Focus on sources like avocados, nuts, and olive oil

Portion Control Tips

  • Use smaller plates
  • Fill half your plate with vegetables
  • Measure portions when cooking
  • Listen to hunger cues

Meal Planning Strategies

Successful healthy eating starts with good planning:

  • Prepare meals in advance
  • Keep healthy snacks readily available
  • Create a weekly meal schedule
  • Shop with a grocery list
