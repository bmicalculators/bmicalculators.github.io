Physical Activity Recommendations

Getting Active for Better Health

Regular physical activity is crucial for maintaining a healthy weight and overall well-being. Learn how to incorporate more movement into your daily routine.

Weekly Activity Goals

  • Moderate Aerobic Activity 150 minutes/week
  • Vigorous Aerobic Activity 75 minutes/week
  • Strength Training 2-3 sessions/week

Types of Physical Activity

Aerobic Activities

  • Walking or jogging
  • Swimming
  • Cycling
  • Dancing

Strength Training

  • Weight lifting
  • Bodyweight exercises
  • Resistance band workouts
  • Yoga

Getting Started Safely

  • Start slowly and gradually increase intensity
  • Warm up before exercise
  • Stay hydrated
  • Listen to your body
  • Consult healthcare provider before starting new exercise routine
