Understanding Nutrition Labels

Reading Food Labels for Better Health

Understanding nutrition labels is key to making informed food choices. Learn how to decode the information on food packaging to support your health goals.

Key Elements of a Nutrition Label

  • Serving Size

    All nutritional information is based on one serving

  • Calories

    Energy content per serving

  • Nutrients

    Amounts of fats, proteins, carbohydrates, and more

  • % Daily Value

    Percentage of daily recommended intake

Important Nutrients to Monitor

Limit These:

  • Saturated Fat
  • Added Sugars
  • Sodium

Look For These:

  • Fiber
  • Protein
  • Vitamins & Minerals

Tips for Label Reading

  • Check serving sizes first
  • Compare similar products
  • Look at the ingredient list
  • Be aware of marketing claims
